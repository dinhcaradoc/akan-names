AKAN NAMES

Hi there and welcome to our Akan Names page. Have you heard of Akan Names? These are traditional Ghanain names that are given to a person based on their gender and the day of the week. Different names are given to a person depending on the day of the week on which they were born. Thus, as long as you know the day of the week on which you were born, then you can easily find your Akan name.

Akan Names List

The list below outlines all fourteen Akan Names, one for each day of the week and each gender. If you know the day of the week on which you were born, then you can simply pick your Akan Name. In case you are not sure about the day of the week on which you were born, you can use our Akan names generator to find your Akan name. The generator is provided at the end of the list.
